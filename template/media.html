Sakshar Launches "Shiksha na Ruke"

Sakshar proudly launches its flagship initiative, "Shiksha na Ruke", aimed at supporting students at risk of dropping out due to financial hardships and lack of access to schools. This initiative works by setting up local learning centers and community-driven mentoring programs.

"She Can Fly" Empowers Girls

"She Can Fly" empowers young girls from marginalized communities with education, digital literacy, scholarships, and leadership training. The program ensures girls are not left behind due to gender or economic inequality, reaching over 500 beneficiaries so far.

Ujjwal Bhavishya Expands to 3 States

"Ujjwal Bhavishya" provides career guidance, vocational training, and job support for youth. The initiative has expanded to 3 states and impacted more than 10,000 students, offering them hope for a brighter future through skill-building and placement opportunities.
